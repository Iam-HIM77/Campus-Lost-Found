About Campus Lost & Found

A digital platform designed to help students recover lost items easily.

Project Overview

Campus Lost & Found is a web-based system that allows students to report lost and found items within the university environment. It helps connect item owners with finders in a fast and organized way.

Our Mission

To reduce the stress of losing personal items on campus by providing a simple, reliable and efficient platform for reporting and claiming items.

Objectives

  • To help students report lost items easily
  • To allow users to report found items
  • To connect item owners with finders
  • To reduce item loss confusion on campus

How It Works

Users register and log in to the system. They can report lost or found items, view items posted by others, and submit claims to verify ownership.
